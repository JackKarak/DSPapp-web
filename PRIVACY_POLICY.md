# Privacy Policy for The DSP App

Last updated: August 30, 2025

## Information We Collect

### Personal Information
- Name and contact information (email, phone number)
- University information (major, graduation year)
- Fraternity-related information (pledge class, officer position)
- Profile information you choose to provide

### Usage Data
- Event attendance records
- Point tracking and achievements
- App usage analytics
- Device information for app functionality

## How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:
- Provide fraternity management services
- Track event attendance and points
- Send notifications about events and announcements
- Improve app functionality and user experience
- Maintain fraternity records and analytics

## Data Storage and Security

- All data is stored securely using Supabase infrastructure
- Data is encrypted both in transit and at rest
- Access is restricted to authorized fraternity officers
- We implement industry-standard security measures

## Information Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal information with third parties except:
- As required by law
- To protect the rights and safety of users
- With your explicit consent
- For essential app functionality (authentication, data storage)

## Your Rights

You have the right to:
- Access your personal data
- Update or correct your information
- Request deletion of your account
- Opt out of non-essential communications

## Data Retention

- Account data is retained while you are an active member
- Event data is retained for historical record keeping
- You may request data deletion by contacting us
- Deleted data is permanently removed within 30 days

## Children's Privacy

This app is not intended for use by individuals under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

## Third-Party Services

This app uses the following third-party services:
- Supabase (data storage and authentication)
- Google Calendar API (event synchronization)
- Expo platform services (app functionality)

## International Data Transfers

Your data may be processed and stored in servers located outside your country. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place to protect your data.
