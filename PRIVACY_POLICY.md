# Privacy Policy for The DSP App
**Delta Sigma Pi Fraternity Mobile Application**

Last updated: October 16, 2025

## Overview

The DSP App is the official mobile application for Delta Sigma Pi fraternity chapters, designed to facilitate member engagement, event management, and organizational communication. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our application.

## Information We Collect

### Personal Information
- **Identity Information**: Full name, email address, phone number, university ID (UID)
- **Academic Information**: Expected graduation year, academic status
- **Fraternity Information**: Membership status (brother, pledge, officer, admin), pledge class, officer position (if applicable)
- **Account Credentials**: Encrypted password and authentication tokens
- **Profile Data**: Any additional information you choose to provide

### Fraternity Activity Data
- **Event Attendance**: Check-in records, event participation history
- **Point System**: Achievement points, ranking within chapter, milestone accomplishments
- **Appeals and Requests**: Attendance appeals, officer approval requests
- **Calendar Integration**: Event synchronization with personal calendars
- **Communication Data**: Newsletter access, in-app notifications

### Technical Information
- **Device Information**: Device type, operating system, app version
- **Usage Analytics**: Feature usage patterns, session duration, error reports
- **Network Data**: IP address, connection type (for security purposes only)

## How We Use Your Information

### Primary Purposes
- **Membership Management**: Verify member identity, maintain accurate chapter rosters
- **Event Coordination**: Track attendance, send event notifications, manage calendar integration
- **Achievement Tracking**: Calculate and display points, rankings, and achievements
- **Communication**: Deliver chapter announcements, newsletters, and important updates
- **Officer Functions**: Provide administrative tools for chapter leadership and event management
- **Account Security**: Authenticate users and protect against unauthorized access

### Secondary Purposes
- **Analytics and Improvement**: Analyze app usage to enhance user experience and functionality
- **Historical Records**: Maintain chapter archives and long-term engagement tracking
- **Support Services**: Provide technical assistance and respond to user inquiries

## Legal Basis for Processing (GDPR Compliance)
- **Legitimate Interest**: Chapter management and member engagement activities
- **Consent**: Newsletter subscriptions and optional features
- **Contractual Necessity**: Account creation and membership verification
- **Legal Obligation**: Compliance with university and organizational requirements

## Data Storage and Security

### Infrastructure
- **Secure Hosting**: All data stored on Supabase's enterprise-grade infrastructure with SOC 2 Type II compliance
- **Encryption**: Data encrypted in transit (TLS 1.3) and at rest (AES-256)
- **Access Controls**: Multi-factor authentication and role-based access restrictions
- **Geographic Storage**: Data stored in secure US-based data centers

### Security Measures
- **Authentication**: Industry-standard password hashing and session management
- **Network Security**: Regular security audits and vulnerability assessments
- **Data Backup**: Automated daily backups with disaster recovery protocols
- **Monitoring**: 24/7 security monitoring and incident response procedures

### Access Restrictions
- **Officer Access**: Limited to authorized chapter officers for legitimate administrative purposes
- **Role-Based Permissions**: Different access levels based on member role (member, officer, admin)
- **Audit Trails**: All data access logged and monitored for unauthorized activity
- **Regular Reviews**: Quarterly access permission reviews and updates

## Information Sharing and Disclosure

### No Third-Party Sales
We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties for marketing purposes.

### Limited Sharing Scenarios
We may share your information only in the following circumstances:
- **Chapter Officers**: Authorized officers may access member data for legitimate fraternity business
- **University Compliance**: When required to meet university reporting or safety requirements
- **Legal Obligations**: When compelled by law, court order, or government agency
- **Safety Concerns**: To protect the safety and welfare of members or prevent illegal activities
- **Service Providers**: With trusted third-party services under strict confidentiality agreements

### Third-Party Services Integration
- **Supabase**: Database hosting and authentication (data processing agreement in place)
- **Google Calendar**: Event synchronization (optional, user-controlled)
- **Expo Platform**: App development and deployment infrastructure
- **Apple/Google**: App distribution through official app stores

### Your Privacy Rights

### Access and Control
- **Data Access**: Request a copy of all personal data we have about you
- **Data Correction**: Update or correct inaccurate information through your profile
- **Data Portability**: Receive your data in a structured, machine-readable format
- **Account Deletion**: Request complete removal of your account and associated data

### Account Deletion Process
Users may permanently delete their account at any time by:
1. **In-App Deletion**: Navigate to Account → Settings → Delete Account
2. **Confirmation Required**: Type "DELETE MY ACCOUNT" to confirm the irreversible action
3. **Immediate Anonymization**: Personal data is anonymized immediately upon deletion
4. **Complete Removal**: All user data is permanently removed from our servers within 30 days
5. **Recovery Period**: Contact support within 7 days if you wish to recover your account

What Gets Deleted:
- Personal information (name, email, phone number)
- Event attendance records
- Points and achievement history
- All appeals and feedback submissions
- Account credentials and authentication tokens
- Communication preferences and notifications

What May Be Retained (Anonymized):
- Aggregated analytics for organizational metrics
- Activity logs for audit and security purposes (marked as deleted user)
- Historical data required for chapter record-keeping (anonymized)

### Communication Preferences
- **Opt-Out Rights**: Unsubscribe from non-essential notifications and newsletters
- **Notification Settings**: Customize which types of alerts you receive
- **Marketing Communications**: Decline promotional materials (chapter events remain active)

### Data Processing Rights (GDPR)
- **Right to Object**: Object to processing based on legitimate interest
- **Right to Restrict**: Limit how we process your data under certain circumstances
- **Right to Rectification**: Correct inaccurate or incomplete data
- **Right to be Forgotten**: Request deletion of personal data when legally permissible

### How to Exercise Your Rights
- **In-App Settings**: Access most privacy controls through your account settings
- **Contact Officer**: Reach out to your chapter's designated privacy officer
- **Email Request**: Send requests to operationsdspgs@gmail.com
- **Response Time**: We will respond to requests within 30 days

## Data Retention Policies

### Active Membership Data
- **Account Information**: Retained while you maintain active membership status
- **Event Records**: Kept for historical tracking and achievement calculation
- **Communication Preferences**: Maintained for the duration of your membership

### Post-Graduation/Departure
- **Alumni Status**: Basic contact information may be retained for alumni relations
- **Historical Records**: Event attendance and achievements may be preserved for chapter archives
- **Complete Deletion**: Available upon request, subject to legal retention requirements

### Automated Deletion
- **Inactive Accounts**: Accounts inactive for 2+ years may be archived or deleted
- **Temporary Data**: Session tokens and cached data deleted automatically
- **Backup Retention**: Deleted data removed from backups within 90 days

### Legal Retention Requirements
Some data may be retained longer to comply with:
- University record-keeping requirements
- Financial and tax obligations
- Legal dispute resolution needs
