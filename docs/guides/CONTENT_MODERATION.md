# Content Moderation Guidelines
**The DSP App Community Standards for Professional Business Fraternity**

Last updated: October 16, 2025

## 1. Overview and Purpose

The DSP App is designed to foster a professional, inclusive, and productive environment for Delta Sigma Pi professional business fraternity members. These Content Moderation Guidelines establish clear standards for acceptable behavior and content within our digital professional community.

### 1.1 Core Professional Principles
- **Professional Excellence**: Maintaining high standards in all interactions and communications
- **Business Ethics**: Demonstrating integrity and ethical behavior in all activities
- **Scholarship**: Promotion of academic and professional learning and development
- **Service**: Encouragement of community involvement and professional giving back
- **Brotherhood**: Respect and support for all members in their professional journeys
- **Career Development**: Focus on mutual advancement and professional growth

### 1.2 Scope of Application
These guidelines apply to all user-generated content within The DSP App, including:
- Professional event comments and discussions
- Profile information and professional descriptions
- Appeal submissions and business-related communications
- Networking interactions and professional exchanges
- Any other user-contributed content or professional interactions

## 2. Professional Content Standards

### 2.1 Encouraged Content
We welcome content that:
- **Celebrates Professional Achievement**: Recognition of career milestones, academic accomplishments, and business successes
- **Promotes Professional Development**: Constructive discussion about career events, workshops, and skill-building activities
- **Shares Business Insights**: Professional knowledge, industry trends, and career advice
- **Fosters Professional Community**: Helpful information, networking opportunities, and supportive professional communications
- **Advances Fraternity Values**: Content aligned with Delta Sigma Pi principles and professional mission
- **Builds Business Networks**: Connections that support career advancement and professional growth

### 2.2 Professional Standards
All content should maintain a level appropriate for:
- Business and professional networking environments
- Corporate and industry representation
- Future career advancement and professional opportunities
- Alumni, employers, and professional contact visibility
- Graduate school and professional program considerations

## 3. Prohibited Content and Behavior

### 3.1 Harassment and Bullying
**Strictly Prohibited:**
- Personal attacks, threats, or intimidation
- Discriminatory language based on race, ethnicity, religion, gender, sexual orientation, or any other protected characteristic
- Cyberbullying or targeted harassment campaigns
- Doxxing or sharing personal information without consent
- Revenge-based content or vindictive behavior

### 3.2 Inappropriate Content
**Not Permitted:**
- Sexually explicit material or suggestive content
- Graphic violence or disturbing imagery
- Content promoting illegal drug use or excessive alcohol consumption
- Gambling or other prohibited activities
- Hate speech or discriminatory content

### 3.3 Business and Professional Misconduct
**Forbidden:**
- False information about professional events, business meetings, or chapter activities
- Impersonation of other members, officers, or business professionals
- Fraudulent attendance claims or professional misrepresentation
- Spreading unverified business rumors or career-damaging information
- Professional dishonesty or unethical business practice facilitation
- Misrepresentation of business credentials or professional achievements

### 3.4 Professional Privacy Violations
**Unacceptable:**
- Sharing other members' professional or personal information without permission
- Screenshots or sharing of private business conversations
- Unauthorized recording of professional events or meetings
- Violation of university, business, or fraternity confidentiality policies
- Disclosure of proprietary business information or trade secrets

### 3.5 Unprofessional Communication and Abuse
**Prohibited:**
- Excessive posting or unprofessional comment spam
- Inappropriate commercial content or unauthorized business advertisements
- Repetitive or disruptive professional messaging
- System abuse or attempts to circumvent professional networking features
- Solicitation of illegal business activities or unethical practices

## 4. Enforcement Mechanisms

### 4.1 Automated Detection
- **Content Filtering**: AI-powered detection of inappropriate language and imagery
- **Pattern Recognition**: Identification of spam, harassment patterns, or unusual activity
- **Real-Time Monitoring**: Continuous scanning of user-generated content
- **Flag Integration**: Automated escalation of potentially problematic content

### 4.2 User Reporting System
- **Easy Reporting**: Simple in-app reporting mechanism for problematic content
- **Anonymous Options**: Protection for users reporting violations
- **Category Selection**: Specific violation types for targeted review
- **Status Updates**: Notifications about report status and outcomes

### 4.3 Manual Review Process
- **Officer Review**: Chapter officers trained in content moderation
- **Escalation Procedures**: Clear pathways for complex or serious violations
- **Context Consideration**: Review of circumstances and intent behind content
- **Fair Assessment**: Balanced evaluation considering all relevant factors

## 5. Progressive Enforcement Actions

### 5.1 Warning System
**First Violation:**
- Educational warning with guideline explanation
- Opportunity for content correction or removal
- Documentation in user record for pattern tracking
- No impact on account standing or privileges

### 5.2 Content Removal
**Repeated or Serious Violations:**
- Immediate removal of offending content
- Notification to user with specific guideline reference
- Required acknowledgment of community standards
- Temporary restriction on posting capabilities

### 5.3 Account Restrictions
**Persistent Violations:**
- **Temporary Suspension**: 24-72 hour access limitation
- **Feature Restriction**: Limited posting, commenting, or interaction abilities
- **Probationary Period**: Enhanced monitoring and restriction of privileges
- **Educational Requirements**: Mandatory review of community guidelines

### 5.4 Account Termination
**Severe or Repeated Violations:**
- Permanent removal from The DSP App
- Notification to chapter leadership
- Potential referral to university disciplinary processes
- Documentation for future reference and chapter records

### 5.5 Appeal Process
- **Right to Appeal**: All enforcement actions may be contested
- **Review Committee**: Neutral review by designated officers or committee
- **Evidence Consideration**: Full review of content, context, and circumstances
- **Timely Resolution**: Appeals processed within 7 business days
- **Final Decision Authority**: Chapter leadership or national organization oversight

## 6. Special Considerations

### 6.1 Professional and Career Impact
Members should consider that:
- Digital professional profiles can significantly affect career opportunities
- Business and corporate policies may apply to professional fraternity-related content
- Professional associations, employers, and industry contacts may review digital presence
- Alumni networks and professional mentors may access posted content
- Content may impact professional licensing, certifications, and business opportunities

### 6.2 Legal, University, and Professional Compliance
- **University Policies**: Adherence to institutional codes of conduct and business school standards
- **Legal Requirements**: Compliance with applicable business laws and professional regulations
- **Title IX Considerations**: Awareness of sexual harassment and discrimination policies in professional settings
- **FERPA Compliance**: Protection of educational records and academic information
- **Professional Ethics**: Adherence to business ethics codes and industry standards
- **Corporate Policies**: Awareness of employer policies regarding professional associations

### 6.3 Emergency Situations
**Immediate Action Required for:**
- Threats of violence or self-harm in professional or personal contexts
- Illegal business activity reporting or promotion
- Safety concerns for members or professional community
- Crisis situations requiring professional or personal intervention
- Unethical business practices that may harm members or the organization

**Emergency Response:**
- Immediate content removal and account suspension
- Notification to appropriate authorities (university, law enforcement, professional boards)
- Crisis intervention and professional support resource provision
- Documentation and follow-up procedures with relevant stakeholders
