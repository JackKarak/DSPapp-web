# Privacy Policy for The DSP App

Last updated: August 30, 2025

## Information We Collect

### Personal Information
- Name and contact information (email, phone number)
- University information (major, graduation year)
- Fraternity-related information (pledge class, officer position)
- Profile information you choose to provide

### Usage Data
- Event attendance records
- Point tracking and achievements
- App usage analytics
- Device information for app functionality

## How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:
- Provide fraternity management services
- Track event attendance and points
- Send notifications about events and announcements
- Improve app functionality and user experience
- Maintain fraternity records and analytics

## Data Storage and Security

- All data is stored securely using Supabase infrastructure
- Data is encrypted both in transit and at rest
- Access is restricted to authorized fraternity officers
- We implement industry-standard security measures

## Information Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal information with third parties except:
- As required by law
- To protect the rights and safety of users
- With your explicit consent
- For essential app functionality (authentication, data storage)

## Your Rights

You have the right to:
- Access your personal data
- Update or correct your information
- Request deletion of your account
- Opt out of non-essential communications

## Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at:
- Email: jkara@umd.edu
- Through the app's feedback system

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy in the app and updating the "Last updated" date.

By using The DSP App, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this Privacy Policy.
