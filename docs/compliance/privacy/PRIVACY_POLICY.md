Privacy Policy
Last updated: October 16th, 2025

Overview
The DSP App is the official mobile application for Delta Sigma Pi fraternity chapters, created to support member engagement, event coordination, and organizational communication.
This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use The DSP App. By using the app, you agree to the practices outlined below.
The DSP App is operated by participating Delta Sigma Pi chapters and managed by authorized student officers. Data is securely hosted through Supabase, a trusted cloud provider.

Information We Collect
Personal Information
Identity: Full name, university email address, phone number, and University ID (UID)
Academic: Graduation year and academic status
Fraternity: Membership status (brother, pledge, officer, admin), pledge class, and officer position (if applicable)
Credentials: Encrypted password and authentication tokens
Profile Data: Any additional information you choose to provide
Fraternity Activity Data
Event attendance, check-ins, and participation history
Point system data (achievements, rankings, milestones)
Attendance appeals and officer approval requests
Event calendar information and notifications
Newsletter and in-app communication data
Technical Information
Device type, operating system, and app version
Usage analytics and error reports
IP address and network type (for security monitoring only)

How We Use Your Information
Primary Uses
Verify and manage member accounts
Track attendance and fraternity participation
Calculate and display points, achievements, and rankings
Send event reminders, updates, and newsletters
Support officer-level administrative functions
Securely authenticate and protect accounts
Secondary Uses
Analyze app performance and improve functionality
Maintain chapter history and records
Provide technical and user support
We do not use personal information for advertising, behavioral profiling, or commercial marketing.

Legal Basis for Processing
Depending on your location, processing may be based on:
Legitimate Interest: Supporting fraternity operations and engagement
Consent: Optional features such as calendar syncing or newsletters
Contractual Necessity: Account creation and membership verification
If you are located in the European Union (EU), your data will be processed in accordance with the principles of the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR).

Data Storage and Security
Hosting: All data is stored on Supabase’s secure U.S.-based servers.
Encryption: Data is encrypted in transit (TLS 1.3) and at rest (AES-256).
Access Controls: Role-based permissions and multi-factor authentication protect against unauthorized access.
Monitoring: Regular security audits, 24/7 monitoring, and automated backups.
While we implement strong safeguards, no online system is entirely secure. Users share information at their own risk, and we encourage strong passwords and device protection.

Information Sharing and Disclosure
We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information.
Information may be shared only in limited circumstances:
Chapter Officers: For legitimate fraternity operations and event management
University Administration: To comply with university safety or reporting requirements
Legal Obligations: When required by law, court order, or government agency
Service Providers: Trusted partners (e.g., Supabase, Expo, Google Calendar) under strict data-processing agreements
Safety Concerns: When necessary to protect the health, safety, or rights of members
We do not share personal data with advertisers or social media platforms.

Your Privacy Rights
You have the right to:
Access your personal data
Correct inaccurate information
Request Deletion of your account and associated data
Limit or object to certain types of processing
Export your data in a portable format
Requests may be made through in-app settings or by emailing operationsdspgs@gmail.com. We may require identity verification before processing requests. Most requests will receive a response within 30 days.

Data Retention
Active Members: Data is retained while membership is active
Alumni: Basic contact and achievement information may be preserved for alumni relations
Inactive Accounts: Archived or deleted after 2 years of inactivity
Deleted Data: Permanently removed from backups within 90 days
Certain information may be retained longer to meet university, financial, or legal requirements.

Age Requirements and Student Privacy
The DSP App is designed for college students aged 18 or older.
We do not knowingly collect data from anyone under 13 years of age (COPPA compliance).
Academic information collected is limited to directory-level data (e.g., graduation year).
We comply with applicable FERPA standards and partner university privacy policies.

Community and Content Standards
Users are expected to uphold the values of Delta Sigma Pi. Inappropriate, harassing, or illegal content is strictly prohibited.
Violations may result in content removal, account suspension, or permanent ban. Members can report inappropriate behavior through the in-app reporting system.
